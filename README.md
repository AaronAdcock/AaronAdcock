# Dr. Aaron Adcock

**Research Engineering Manager at Meta AI (FAIR)** | **Stanford Ph.D.**

---

### 🛡️ Professional Profile

Dr. Aaron Adcock is a Research Engineering Manager at Meta's Fundamental AI Research (FAIR) organization. He leads high-impact teams at the intersection of large-scale AI infrastructure and fundamental research. His leadership is pivotal in advancing the next generation of foundation models, focusing on building scalable, fair, and efficient machine learning systems that bridge the gap between theoretical breakthroughs and production-grade AI infrastructure.

With a Ph.D. from Stanford University in Electrical Engineering (Computational and Mathematical Engineering), Aaron's expertise spans Topological Data Analysis (TDA), Social Network Analysis, and Distributed Systems optimization. He has been instrumental in major FAIR initiatives, including the Segment Anything Model (SAM), Masked Autoencoders (MAE), and FACET (FAirness in Computer Vision EvaluaTion).
